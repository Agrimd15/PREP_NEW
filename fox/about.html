Our Purpose

Although we serve as an educational platform, our purpose is more substantial than merely providing kids with free schooling. We are striving as a group to bring together our community as a whole through shared interests. To bridge the different extremities of our society, we are attempting to put together a platform that unites our next generations and provides them with the foundation and preparation they need based on our prior experiences.

To do this, we are putting together a series of classes and workshops that are taught in school curriculums worldwide. The purpose of this is not only to give our students a strong foundation and conceptual basis but to prepare them for their selective futures by teaching them various approaches to unique problems. We designed workshops to incorporate both academics and extracurriculars, where your student is paired with a mentor who specializes in the chosen subject.

Our program primarily targets kids who are in the Elementary/Middle School Range. As a community project, we are looking forward to meeting new faces and grow together through this process.

Our Platform

Prepology is an academic platform that aims to unite the students of our community. We offer online workshops with custom curriculums which target to build a strong foundation for our studentsâ€™ fundamentals. These workshops are flexible, but categorize primarily around Mathematics and Science. If you are not interested in tutoring and/or virtual learning, we host competitions where the winner earns themselves a special prize. These competitions include virtual Science Fairs and Math Olympiad-type contests. In order to benefit our community, we plan to take advantage of our resources, and offer these services for free. This is so that the ones who lack opportunity and motivation will be inspired to join as well. The COVID-19 Crisis only furthers our incitement, as it is slowly pushing us to provide individuals with the best versions of themselves; one step at a time.
